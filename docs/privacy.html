Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Daily Wisdom & Faith Auto-Upload App ("the App") handles information.

1. Who operates this app

The App is operated by Naveen Dsouza, owner of the Daily Wisdom & Faith YouTube channel (naveends798@gmail.com). This is a personal, single-user application — not a public service.

2. What data the app accesses

The App accesses the following data using Google APIs:

  • YouTube channel data — to upload videos to the owner's own YouTube channel using the YouTube Data API v3.
  • Google Drive files — to read video files that the App itself generated and stored in the owner's own Google Drive.

The App does not collect, store, or share data from any third party. All data accessed belongs solely to the App owner.

3. How data is used

Data is used exclusively to automate the daily upload of gospel devotional videos to the owner's YouTube channel. No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising.

4. Data storage

OAuth credentials (access tokens) are stored locally on the owner's personal computer and are never transmitted to any third-party server.

5. Third-party services

The App uses the following Google services under their respective terms:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • YouTube API Services Terms of Service

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: naveends798@gmail.com
