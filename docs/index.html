Daily Wisdom & Faith

Auto-Upload App — a personal tool that automatically publishes daily gospel devotional videos to the Daily Wisdom & Faith YouTube channel.

What this app does

  • Generates a short daily devotional video (scripture, reflection, and music)
  • Uploads the video directly to the Daily Wisdom & Faith YouTube channel
  • Runs automatically once per day — no manual work required

Who uses this app

This app is used solely by the channel owner (Naveen Dsouza) to manage their own YouTube channel. It is not a public service and does not process data from any third parties.

Permissions used

  • YouTube Data API (upload scope) — to upload videos to the owner's own channel
  • Google Drive API (read scope) — to read generated video files stored in the owner's own Google Drive
